Instructions For The Game
  • Your have to slice colourful balls by Right Click and Drag
  • Black Coloured balls are Bombs slicing them will decrease score by 6 points
  • Slicing colourful balls will give 10 point each
  • You get 10 lives in the game which decrease by slicing black balls
  • Game stops when timer completes it's 2min or you loss all your lives
